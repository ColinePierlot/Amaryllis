Our story

Amaryllis was founded in 2021 by 4 students of the IÉSEG School of Management for a Digital Technology group project. They wanted to invent a small business with big dreams. The objective was simple : to create a completely inclusive and sustainable jewelry store exclusively online. Amaryllis values singularity and originality but also creativity, inclusion, sustainability, and authenticity ng your are looking for! :-)

Our products

Amaryllis’ products are completely made with sustainable materials. We are engaged in recycling and reusing materials. We use a 100% sustainable packaging through eco-friendly shipping. Our products are also promoting inclusion though unisex jewelry so that everyone feels united and unique at the same time.
